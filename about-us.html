About Us

We are a group of students and young developers passionate about web design and development. This portfolio platform was created as part of our learning journey to combine creativity and technical skills into one practical project. The idea behind this project came from a common problem: many people struggle to present their skills and work in a professional and organized way. To solve this, we built a modern web application that allows users to easily create their own portfolios, choose templates, and generate shareable links. This project helps us improve our skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript while also teaching us teamwork, structure, and real-world web development concepts. More than just a project, it represents our passion, creativity, and growth as developers.
